What We Offer

Our Services

Specialist end-to-end delivery for RE wall systems and road construction — from casting yard to completed wall.

Service 01
Casting of RE Panels

Our in-house casting facility produces Reinforced Earth facing panels using precision steel proprietary moulds. Every batch is produced with M40/M45 grade concrete, certified rebar cages, and full wet curing before quality sign-off. Panels are stacked, marked, and dispatched to site on schedule.

  • Dedicated casting yard with steel proprietary moulds
  • M40 / M45 grade concrete per project specification
  • Rebar cage fabrication, assembly, and placement
  • Wet curing, visual and dimensional inspection
  • Batch records and material test documentation
  • Organised stacking, marking, and site dispatch
Service 02
Erection of RE Panels

Expert installation of RE wall panels using Escorts Hydra 14 and ACE mobile cranes operated by certified riggers. We ensure precise plumb and level placement for every panel, correct geogrid layer installation at design-specified intervals, engineered fill compaction, and final structural alignment sign-off.

  • Escorts Hydra 14 and ACE 14XW crane operations
  • Certified riggers and trained installation crew
  • Panel plumb and level setting with survey instruments
  • Geogrid installation at specified spacing intervals
  • Engineered fill placement and compaction testing
  • Final alignment, coping, and structural sign-off
Service 03
Level Pad Construction

We construct precision PCC level pad foundations at the base of RE walls. These pads are critical to correct panel bearing and long-term structural performance. Our surveying teams use laser levels to ensure the pad is set at exactly the specified elevation before any panel is erected.

  • Trench excavation to design depth and width
  • Surveying and laser level marking
  • PCC level pad casting with correct grade concrete
  • Elevation set to design drawing specification
  • Curing and inspection record before erection begins
Service 04
Road Formation & Earthwork

Complete road formation services including bulk earthwork, embankment construction, and subgrade preparation. We operate motorgraders, vibratory compactors, and tippers to build stable road formations ready to receive pavement layers. Compaction is tested and documented to specified Proctor density.

  • Bulk earthwork, cut, and fill operations
  • Embankment construction and shaping
  • Motorgrader profiling — camber and cross-fall
  • Compaction to specified Modified Proctor density
  • Subgrade trimming and level check
Service 05
Panel Transport & Logistics

We manage the complete logistics chain from casting yard to site — transporting heavy RE panels on low-bed trailers, coordinating route surveys, managing oversized load permits, and organising on-site panel stacking and sequencing for efficient erection.

  • Low-bed trailer transport for RE panels
  • Route survey and over-dimensional load permits
  • On-site panel numbering, stacking, and management
  • Aggregate, fill, and concrete material haulage
  • Equipment mobilisation and de-mobilisation
Service 06
Project Management & QA

Our site engineers and project managers oversee every stage of construction with rigorous QA/QC procedures. We maintain complete documentation — material test certificates, pour records, inspection checklists, and progress photographs — all organised for client submission and record.

  • Dedicated site engineer and supervisors on each project
  • Daily progress logs and site photography
  • QA/QC documentation and checklists
  • Third-party laboratory test coordination
  • Weekly client progress reports
  • As-built records and DPR submission
